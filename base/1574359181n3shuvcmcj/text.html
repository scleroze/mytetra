/* Definitions of useful mathematical constants

* M_E - e

* M_LOG2E - log2(e)

* M_LOG10E - log10(e)

* M_LN10 - ln(10)

* M_PI - pi

* M_PI_2 - pi/2

* M_PI_4 - pi/4

* M_1_PI - 1/pi

* M_2_PI - 2/pi

* M_2_SQRTPI - 2/sqrt(pi)

* M_SQRT2 - sqrt(2)

* M_SQRT1_2 - 1/sqrt(2)

*/
